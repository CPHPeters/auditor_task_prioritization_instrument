Agrifirm calculated the fair value estimates by using a Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) model. The engagement partner and Agrifirm's CFO agrees that the DCF model is the most applicable model. Agrifirm receives royalty payments, these are cash flows calculated as the royaltypercentage times the machine-specific revenues of CMG Machines. Agrifirm values the patent by using the net present value of these royalty payments. The Discounted Cash Flow model is calculated as follows:

DCF = CF1/(1+r)1 + CF2/(1+r)2 + CF3/(1+r)3 + ... + CFn/(1+r)n

where CF are the royalty payments and r is the discount rate.
